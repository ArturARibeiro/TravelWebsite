About Focus

At Focus we were always traveling and we noticed the same problems, time and unforeseen circumstances, when we came together to form the company we already had a clear objective, to make people have the best experience possible and make the most of their trips so we created plans for that everyone enjoy even business trips and we are proud of that.

The focus project came to transform and improve the travel experience of our users. With tailor-made plans for all types of travel, we offer amenities for you and your family to travel and enjoy without worries. For those who need to make a lot of business trips, we offer an executive plan where you can travel whenever you need to and stay at one of our partner hotels. We still have our Master plan, which can be customized your way, making it perfect for your needs.
